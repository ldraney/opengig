Why opengig?

Zero Fees

We never take a cut. You negotiate directly, pay directly. Your money stays yours.

AI-Powered

Natural language search and matching. Just describe what you need - no forms to fill.

LinkedIn Trust

All users verified via LinkedIn OAuth. Real identities, email verified. No fake profiles.

Terminal-Native

Built for developers. Runs in your terminal via Claude Code. Fast and keyboard-driven.

Open Source

Privacy First

2

Talk

4

Connect

Message people, build rapport, and share contact info when you're ready.
